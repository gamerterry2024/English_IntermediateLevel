The Direct Processing System

Master Logic

Core Objective

This system is designed to prevent "mental freezing" and ensure real-time comprehension by prioritizing the Subject-Verb connection above all else.

STEP 1. Dropping the Anchor (Saving the Core Structure)

Identify the first meaningful chunk to secure the Subject.

  • Case OK: Meaning is clear? → Memorize the Subject and drive forward.
  • Case FAIL: Meaning is unclear? → Don't get stuck. Find the Main Noun (Subject), define it, and hold it until the verb appears.

STEP 2. Selective Visualization (Modifiers)

Process the next chunks (excluding the Subject).

  • Case OK: Meaning clear? → Quickly visualize the image and proceed.
  • Case FAIL: Meaning unclear? → Skip it immediately. Do not stop the "movie."

STEP 3. The Great Match (Connecting to the Verb)

Repeat Step 2 until you hit the Main Verb.

Connect the "Main Verb" instantly to the "Subject" stored in Step 1.

*Logic: Every main verb must be paired with its anchored subject to complete the thought.

STEP 4. Final Processing

Continue Step 2 for any remaining modifiers to wrap up the sentence.
