Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 09-12

Unit 09: Nature & Science

Zero/1st Conditionals for facts/possibilities. Articles (the/zero) for geography and abstract nouns.

Unit 10: Education & Habits

Present Perfect Continuous for ongoing duration. Used to for past states/habits no longer true.

Unit 11: Places & Services

Passive Voice (be + past participle). Hotel and travel vocabulary: amenities, reservation, reception.

Unit 12: Communication

Verbs + -ing / to-infinitive. Phrasal verbs: pick up, switch off, call back. Technology usage.
